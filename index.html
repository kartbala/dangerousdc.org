Making DC Streets Safer Through Documentation

DangerousDC.org is a citizen-led initiative documenting safety violations throughout Washington, DC. We photograph and catalog illegal sign placements, sightline violations, broken sidewalks, and other infrastructure issues that make our streets less safe for pedestrians, cyclists, and drivers.

Our goal is to raise awareness and provide documented evidence to help city officials prioritize repairs and enforcement.

About This Project

DangerousDC.org was created by concerned DC residents who believe that systematic documentation of safety violations can help prioritize city resources and improve enforcement. We work to:

  • Create a public record of recurring safety issues
  • Provide evidence for 311 and agency reports
  • Raise community awareness about safety concerns
  • Support data-driven policy decisions

This is a non-partisan, community safety initiative.
